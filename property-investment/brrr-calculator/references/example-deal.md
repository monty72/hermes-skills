# Example Deal — St. Anns Road, Rotherham S65

This is a worked example of the BRRR calculator for reference.

## Inputs
- Purchase: £70,000 (offer, list £80k, 3 months stale)
- Refurb: £10,000 (+10% contingency)
- ARV: £120,000
- Rent: £700/mo
- Structure: SPV

## Output

## Key Takeaways

- Offering £70k on an £80k list (3 months stale) makes the BRRR work — all capital recycled + £1,120 surplus
- At the full £80k asking, £4,300 stays trapped in the deal and cashflow is negative
- SPV wins over personal: personal ownership at 40% tax gives -£94/mo after S24 tax penalty
- 30yr term improves cashflow vs 25yr for SPV BTL
- Retained equity of £31k on zero cash = immediate wealth creation
