## What Axle Energy Supports (for VPP referral)

Axle works at the **inverter/battery hardware level** via direct communication protocols (Modbus, CAN bus). They cannot control cloud-only APIs like Tesla's.

### Compatible Batteries (2026)
- **Solis** — hybrid inverter + battery systems
- **SolaX** — SolaX inverters + batteries
- **FoxESS** — FoxESS batteries
- **GivEnergy** — GivEnergy batteries
- **SolarEdge** — SolarEdge inverters + batteries
- "More brands coming soon — request yours" button available on their landing page

### NOT supported
- **Tesla Powerwall** (any version) — proprietary protocol, no third-party VPP API
- **Tesla Powerwall owners** can use Octopus Flux (export up to 15p/kWh) or Octopus Saving Sessions (DFS during winter) instead

### Axle Installer Referral Program
- Installers get **£50 per successful enrolment** of a customer's compatible battery
- Customers earn £1/kWh + £10/month minimum (until March 2026)
