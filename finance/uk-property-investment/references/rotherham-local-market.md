## SPV Setup Costs for Rotherham Investment

For a BRRR purchased through a limited company (recommended for higher-rate taxpayers):

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Incorporation (Companies House) | £100 one-off |
| Confirmation statement | £50/yr |
| Accountant (ForeTwo, 1-2 props) | £60/mo (£720/yr) |
| Business bank account (Starling) | £0 |
| Extra mortgage interest (SPV premium ~0.75%) | ~£675/yr on £90k |
| **Total recurring cost** | **~£1,445/yr** |

### Recommended Accountants
- **ForeTwo Accounting** — £60/mo for 1-2 properties, includes FreeAgent, CT returns, SA return, registered office. Best value.
- **Provestor** — £30-80/mo depending on tier. Property-specific platform with bank feeds.
- Local accountant — £450-900/yr but may lack property SPV expertise.

### Mortgage Brokers for SPVs
- Fox Davidson (Bristol-based, national)
- Specialist lenders: Together, Fleet Mortgages, Aldermore, Foundation Home Loans

Bridging finance first (0.5-0.8%/mo, 6-12 month term), then refinance to BTL on the new higher valuation.
