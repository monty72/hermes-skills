# Agent Communication Templates

Use these when communicating with UK estate agents on the user's behalf via WhatsApp (wacli) or other channels.

## Leasehold Enquiry

> "Hi [agent], quick one on [address] — could you confirm the remaining lease length, annual ground rent, and whether there are any restrictions on subletting? Thanks."

## Offer — Standard (property on market < 1 month)

> "We'd like to put in an offer of £[X] on [address]. No chain, can complete in [X] weeks. Could you send over the legal pack?"

## Offer — Negotiated (property stale 3+ months)

> "The property's been on the market for [X] months now. We can offer £[10-15% below asking] with a fast completion. No chain."

## Offer — Heavy Negotiation (property stale 5+ months, flagged as investment)

> "Given the property has been listed since [date] and is marketed to investors/speculators, we're prepared to offer £[15-25% below asking]. We can complete within [X] weeks and have finance in place."

## Counter Offer Response

> "We'll meet at £[X] — that's our final figure. We have the funds ready and can move quickly if you can get the seller to agree."

## Legal Pack Request

> "Thanks — could you email the seller's information form, leasehold pack, and any EPC certificate to [user email or forwarded]? We'll review and come back to you."

## Viewing Request

> "Could we arrange a viewing for [property address]? [Day] at [time] works. Let me know what slots the seller has available."

## Quick Confirmation

> "Thanks for the update. We'll proceed on that basis — let me know when the legal pack is ready."
