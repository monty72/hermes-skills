### Merchant of Record (Tax Handling)

- **Gumroad** (MoR since Jan 2025) — Handles global sales tax/VAT. Biggest creator-facing MoR.
- **LemonSqueezy / Polar / Dodo / Paddle / Fungies** — Full MoR. They are the legal seller. Handle global VAT, GST, sales tax. You file nothing.
- **Payhip** — Handles EU & UK VAT (acts as reseller). You handle US sales tax and other jurisdictions manually.
- **Etsy** — Handles EU VAT. You handle US sales tax if you pass thresholds.

## Key Tax/VAT Notes

- UK digital products: 20% VAT on sales to UK consumers (handled by MoR platforms)
- EU digital products: VAT at local rate (handled by MoR platforms)
- US digital products: No VAT, but state sales tax may apply (handle yourself unless on MoR)
- MoR (Merchant of Record) platforms handle ALL tax: collection, remittance, filing
- Non-MoR platforms (Payhip < EU/UK, Ko-fi): YOU are responsible for tax compliance
