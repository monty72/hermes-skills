# Platform Fee Comparison

Effective fees on a $25 product (typical price point):

| Platform | Headline Fee | Processing | Effective ($25) | Keeps Seller |
|----------|-------------|------------|-----------------|--------------|
| Gumroad | 10% + $0.50 | Included | ~20.9% | ~$19.80 |
| LemonSqueezy | 5% + $0.50 | Included | ~7% | ~$23.25 |
| Payhip Free | 5% | ~2.9%+$0.30 | ~10.9% | ~$22.28 |
| Payhip Pro ($29/mo) | 2% | ~2.9%+$0.30 | ~7.9% | ~$23.03 |
| Polar.sh Starter | 5% + $0.50 | Included | ~7% | ~$23.25 |
| Dodo Payments | 4% + $0.40 | +1.5% intl | ~6-7.5% | ~$23.13 |
| Whop | 3% | ~2.7% | ~5.7% | ~$23.58 |

Payhip Pro's $99/mo fee hurts at low revenue but becomes cheapest above ~$2,000/mo.

## Merchant of Record (Tax Handling)

| Platform | Tax Handling |
|----------|-------------|
| Gumroad | MoR since Jan 2025 — global sales tax/VAT handled automatically |
| LemonSqueezy | Full MoR — they are the legal seller. Handle 100+ countries |
| Polar.sh | Full MoR — handles global tax |
| Payhip | EU & UK VAT only (acts as reseller). US sales tax handled manually |
| Etsy | Handles EU VAT. US tax if thresholds met |
| Paddle | Full MoR — enterprise grade, complex onboarding |

## Key Differences

- **Email ownership:** All major platforms allow email export EXCEPT Etsy (you don't own customer emails)
- **Payout schedule:** Gumroad weekly/monthly (min $100), LemonSqueezy twice monthly (min $50), Payhip instant via Stripe
- **Built-in discovery:** Gumroad Discover (but 30% fee), Etsy (95M buyers), none on LemonSqueezy/Payhip
- **API/developer tools:** LemonSqueezy has the best API (license keys, subscriptions). Payhip has no API.

## Recommendation for UK Creator

1. Start with **LemonSqueezy** (5%+$0.50, global tax handled, best API)
2. List on **Etsy** for discovery ($0.20/listing fee)
3. Graduate to **Payhip Pro** when revenue exceeds £1,500/mo
4. Consider **Polar.sh** if building open-source developer tools
